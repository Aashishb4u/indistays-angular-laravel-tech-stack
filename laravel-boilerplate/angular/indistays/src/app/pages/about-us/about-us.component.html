Our Story
Indistays was born from a passion for merging nature's beauty with the luxury of modern comfort. Our journey began with a simple idea: to redefine camping as an experience that leaves you in awe of both the great outdoors and the finer things in life. We wanted to create a space where adventure and relaxation go hand in hand, and where every moment feels like a cherished memory.
Luxury in Nature
At Indistays, we curate exceptional camping experiences that transcend the ordinary. We handpick destinations that encapsulate the essence of nature's beauty and tranquility, offering you an escape from the mundane. From the enchanting natural waterfall of Bhandardara to the sun-kissed beaches of Alibaug, and the serene shores of Pavna Lake, each of our carefully selected locations serves as a canvas for your unique adventure.
